Fire Poppers

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups of Brown Sugar
  • 2 Cups of Frank's Red Hot Sauce
  • 2 Cups of Honey
  • Oil (for frying)
  • 1 Cup of Flour
  • Garlic Powder
  • Salt
  • Eggs
  • 4 lb Chicken Cutlets

Steps:

  1. Dip chicken into flour seasoned with garlic and salt then into beaten egg then again into flour mixture.
  2. Heat up oil in a pan.
  3. Fry the nuggets in a generous amount of oil until golden brown and place into a pan draining off all excess oil.
  4. To prepare sauce mix together brown sugar, honey, and hot sauce in a saucepan and heat, stirring until it bubbles.
  5. Remove from heat when bubbling.
  6. Pour sauce onto the nuggets and serve hot.
Enjoy!
